# ABEA - A Bandeira do Elefante e da Arara

This is the Foundry VTT system for the **A Bandeira do Elefante e da Arara** (ABEA) RPG.

## System Overview
The ABEA system is designed to provide a rich and immersive experience for players and Game Masters in the world of A Bandeira do Elefante e da Arara.

## Compatibility
- **Foundry VTT**: Version 13 (build 351)
- **Minimum Version**: 13

## Installation
1. Open Foundry VTT.
2. Go to the **Game Systems** tab.
3. Click **Install System**.
4. Search for "ABEA" or paste the manifest URL: `https://raw.githubusercontent.com/rafa-pc/abea/main/system.json`.

## Development Guidelines
